How it works

LINE us your photos and we'll handle the rest

1. Sign up Sign up, connect with our LINE channel, and invite your siblings.
2. Send photos There is no app to download. Just LINE message us your photos. You control what photos go to print on our website.
3. Deliver love Every month, we automatically print and ship postcard prints to your loved one.
Prints so good you'll make the fridge

Postcard Prints

Our postcard prints are 33% bigger than a typical smartphone screen — easier on the eyes for folks who wear glasses. They're perfect for the fridge, frames, and albums for reminders of love all around the home. They're photo-lab quality on Kodak and Fuji paper.

Send Photos Together

Unlimited family members

All plans offer unlimited members, so you can invite your whole family — no matter how big and crazy. Just share your FamiFoto Line contact and they can start sending photos in a snap.

Never miss a moment

Reminder Texts

Get scheduled reminders 3 days ahead of your ship date to make sure your loved one never misses a moment.

Tell a story with photo captions

Photo Captions

Send text along with your photo and it'll be added as a caption to the print. It’s a great way to add context to your moments or even share an entire story.
